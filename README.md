# Global Startup Funding Unicorn Analysis (2018-2021)

##  Project Overview
An end-to-end analysis of Global Unicorn Companies (valuation > $1B) from 2018 to 2021, examining growth trends, geographical distribution, industry leaders, and valuation evolution.

##  Research Objectives
1. Analyze the growth trend of 'Unicorn' status companies
2. Identify geographical and industrial leaders
3. Track average valuation evolution over time
4. Categorize companies into estimated funding stages

# Key Findings
Exponential Growth: Surge in unicorn creation between 2020-2021
Market Leaders: USA dominates, with India and UK growing rapidly
Sector Dominance: Fintech and Internet Software lead
Correlation: Negative correlation (-0.32) between founding year and valuation
